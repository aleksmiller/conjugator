Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Conjugator?

Simply download the app from your preferred platform (iOS, Android, or Web), create an account, and select the language you want to learn. You can start practicing verb conjugations immediately!

Which languages are supported?

Conjugator currently supports Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, and more languages are being added regularly. Check the app for the most up-to-date list of available languages.

Is my progress synced across devices?

Yes! When you create an account, your learning progress and preferences are automatically synced across all your devices. You can seamlessly switch between your phone, tablet, and computer.

How much does Conjugator cost?

Conjugator offers a free version with basic features. Premium subscriptions unlock advanced features like offline mode, unlimited practice exercises, and detailed progress analytics. Visit the app for current pricing.

Can I use Conjugator offline?

Premium subscribers can download conjugation tables and practice exercises for offline use. The free version requires an internet connection.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the app settings menu under "Account Management." Please note that this action is permanent and will delete all your progress data.

I found a bug or error. How can I report it?

We appreciate your help in improving Conjugator! Please use the contact form below or email us with details about the issue you encountered.
